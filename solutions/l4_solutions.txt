Cell (Exercise) — CountVectorizer with constraints

Insert this into the first empty code cell right after the “CountVectorizer …” exercise text.

from sklearn.feature_extraction.text import CountVectorizer

vectorizer = CountVectorizer(
    stop_words="english",
    min_df=5,          # at least 5 documents
    max_df=0.30,       # at most 30% of documents
    token_pattern=r"[a-zA-Z]{4,10}",  # letters only, length 4..10 (hint)
    lowercase=True
)

X_bag_of_words = vectorizer.fit_transform(data)
